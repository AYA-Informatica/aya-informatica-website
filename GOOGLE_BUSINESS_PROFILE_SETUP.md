## Step 3: Verification

Google will verify your business via:

### Option 1: Postcard (Most Common)
- Google mails a postcard to your address
- Contains verification code
- Takes 5-14 days
- Enter code when received

### Option 2: Phone/SMS (If Available)
- Instant verification
- Not always available for new businesses

### Option 3: Email (Rare)
- Only for some business types
- Check if available

### Option 4: Video Verification (New)
- Record video of your business location
- Faster than postcard
- May be available for your location
